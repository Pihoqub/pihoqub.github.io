My Games

Here is a list of games that I have made. Click any of them to read more about them.

Arcane Raiders

The rogue-like. dungeon-raider where you and your wizard friends team up to pillage the magic tower!

Created for UTS Game Design Studio 1.

Pascal's Requiem

2.5D adventure RPG with turn-based minigame combat where you must uncover the truth of the future.

Made with the UTS Playmakers Development Team.

Modline Drift

Multiplayer racing game where you use the items found on the track to upgrade your kart or throw them at other players.

Created for UTS Game Design Studio 2.

Super Boxing

Prove yourself as the champion in this recreation of the original boxing game for the Atari 2600!

Created for UTS Game Design Studio 1. 

Climate Clinic

Diagnose and save the animals impacted by climate change as the veterinarian in Climate Clinic.

Created for the Ubisoft 2024 Game Jam.

TAS Bot

Fast paced 2D platformer where you jump, swing, and bounce to your destination in the most battery-efficient way possible.

Created for UTS Game Design Methodologies.
